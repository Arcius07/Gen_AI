Football is the most popular sport in the world. It is a team sport played with a ball between two teams of 11 players. The game is played on a rectangular field called a pitch. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing goal. A match lasts for 90 minutes, split into two 45-minute halves.

The World Cup is the most famous international football tournament. It is organized by FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association). The tournament takes place every four years. Brazil has won the World Cup 5 times, which is the most. Germany and Italy have each won it 4 times. Argentina, winners in 2022, have 3 titles.

Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo are two of the greatest players in the history of the sport. Messi, from Argentina, has won the Ballon d'Or award 8 times, a record. He is known for his incredible dribbling, vision, and goal-scoring. He spent most of his career at Barcelona before moving to Paris Saint-Germain and then Inter Miami.

Cristiano Ronaldo, from Portugal, has won the Ballon d'Or 5 times. He is famous for his athleticism, powerful shooting, and incredible goal-scoring record. He has played for major clubs including Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al-Nassr. He is the all-time top goalscorer in men's international football.

The UEFA Champions League is the top club competition in Europe. It is held annually by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA). Real Madrid is the most successful club in the competition's history, having won the title 15 times. AC Milan is second with 7 titles, followed by Liverpool and Bayern Munich with 6 titles each.

In football, different positions have different roles. The goalkeeper is the only player allowed to use their hands within their own penalty area. Defenders, like center-backs and full-backs, try to stop the opposing team from scoring. Midfielders, such as central or attacking midfielders, link the defense and attack. Forwards, or strikers, are the primary goal-scorers for the team.

Famous managers also leave their mark on the game. Pep Guardiola is known for his "tiki-taka" style of possession-based football, which he used successfully at Barcelona, Bayern Munich, and Manchester City. Jürgen Klopp is famous for his "Gegenpressing" system, a high-intensity counter-pressing style, which brought success to Borussia Dortmund and Liverpool.

The English Premier League is one of the most-watched sports leagues in the world. It consists of 20 teams who play each other home and away. The season runs from August to May. Teams that finish in the top four qualify for the next season's Champions League. The three teams at the bottom of the table are relegated to the division below, the Championship.

Football terminology includes many phrases. An "offside" is a complex rule that prevents players from waiting too close to the opponent's goal. A "hat-trick" is when a player scores three goals in a single game. A "clean sheet" is when a goalkeeper and their team finish a match without conceding any goals.
